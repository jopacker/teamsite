If you’re guessing, you can get it wrong

We aim to understand our users first by doing research, before actually designing and building something, which might
otherwise be based on guesses and which often turns out to be wrong. This process is called user-centred design.

Qualitative research

We aim to get and idea of people’s behaviours and habits so we can truly design the right tool for them. We use diary studies, in depth interviews, observations and workshops.
